The™ Reading Consortium

We are a group of friends who meet regularly to discuss reading, typically about programming or mathematics. This group grew/is growing out of a Haskell special topics course. After the course ended, we agreed that we wanted to keep going, albeit at a reduced pace.

Right now, we are reading Haskell Programming from First Principles. Take a look at the reading schedule!

A tentative topic schedule is available. We plan to cover Rust next; if you have ideas for recommended reading, we'd love to hear!

Want to read about how we run meetings or possible reading topics? You'll find all that here. If you have ideas, feel free to contribute them to that document or in our IRC channel!

If any of this sounds interesting, punch in your email here, and we'll be in touch! You can expect an email in June 2019.

If you have ideas, questions, or just want to chat, you can find us hanging out in #monads on irc.wobscale.website or on Matrix at #monads:wobscale.chat.
